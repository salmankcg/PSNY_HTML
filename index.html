About us

Plastic Surgery of New York delivers outstanding aesthetic results in a luxurious and modern setting. Our elite team of board-certified surgeons are the best at what they do, delivering your cosmetic vision to the highest standard. With over 20 years of experience, we provide a comprehensive range of procedures including facelifts, liposuction, rhinoplasty and breast surgery. Before, during, and after your procedure our attentive care makes you feel as good as you look and you look exceptional.
